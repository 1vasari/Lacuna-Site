Privacy Policy

This policy covers how Lacuna Expanse Corp ("LEC") treats personal information that LEC collects and receives, including information related to your past use of LEC products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable such as your name, address, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available.

Information Collection and Use

LEC collects personal information when you register with LEC, when you use LEC products or services, and when you visit LEC pages or the pages of certain LEC partners. LEC may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from business partners or other companies.

When you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, zip code etc. Once you register with LEC and sign in to our services, you are not anonymous to us.

LEC collects information about your transactions with us and with some of our business partners.

LEC automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, LEC cookie information, and the page you request.

LEC uses information for the following general purposes: to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you, conduct research, and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external clients.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

LEC does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or under the following circumstances:

  • We provide the information to trusted partners who work on behalf of or with LEC under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to help LEC communicate with you about offers from LEC and our marketing partners. However, these companies do not have any independent right to share this information.
  • We respond to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.
  • We believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of LEC's terms of use, or as otherwise required by law.
  • We transfer information about you if LEC is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, LEC will notify you before information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Cookies

LEC may set and access LEC cookies on your computer.

Communications from LEC

If you provide us with your e-mail address, we may send you e-mail from time to time. All e-mails from LEC will be strictly 'opt-out' communications, and will include a URL or email address where you can unsubscribe from future mailings.

Confidentiality and Security

Your LEC Account Information is password-protected.

In certain areas LEC uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect data transmissions. In some cases, at the request of our users, we provide an option to use SSL or not. The default is always to use SSL, but if you choose to not use SSL you do so at your own risk.

We take security seriously and make our best efforts to prevent compromise of your personal information. In the event of a serious security breach, we will alert our registered users.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

LEC may update this policy. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the email address specified in your LEC account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
